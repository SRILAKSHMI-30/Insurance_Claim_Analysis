# 🛡️ Insurance Claim Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project provides a comprehensive **insurance claim performance analysis** through an interactive dashboard.  
It consolidates metrics such as **claims count, profit, customer base, ratings, and regional distributions**, enabling stakeholders to monitor claim efficiency and profitability across categories, states, and regions.

---

## 🔑 Key Features
- **Summary Metrics**
  - Quantity: 13K  
  - Profit: 2.83M  
  - Customers: 1.253K  
  - Status Count: 1,253 (out of 2,506)  

- **Claim Amount by Year**
  - 2023: 1.17M  
  - 2024: 1.98M  
  - 2025: 2.17M  
  - 2026: 0.98M  

- **Regional Distribution**
  - North: 57.14%  
  - East: 32.86%  
  - South: 10%  

- **Ratings by Category & State**
  - States analyzed: Indiana, Louisiana, North Dakota, Wisconsin, Wyoming  
  - Categories: A, B, C, D  

- **Top 5 Cities by Profit**
  - North David: 10.0K  
  - New Stephanie: 9.0K  
  - Lake Jonathan City: 8.6K  
  - New Valerie: 7.9K  
  - New Kenneth: 7.4K  

- **Status Count by Category & Sub-Category**
  - Cancelled, Completed, Pending claims tracked across categories A–D  
  - Profit ranges: 0.22M – 0.28M  

---

## 📈 Insights
- **Peak Claim Year:** 2025 with 2.17M claim amount.  
- **Regional Leader:** North region dominates with 57% of claims.  
- **City Highlight:** North David generates the highest profit among cities.  
- **Trend:** Claim amounts grew steadily until 2025, followed by a decline in 2026.  
- **Category Performance:** Profit distribution across categories remains balanced, with slight variations.
